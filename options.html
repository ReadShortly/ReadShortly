Privacy Information

Your data privacy is important to us.

Read Shortly by CSBD does not save or store any of your content or summarized text in our database. All text processing happens directly through the Google Gemini API.

When you use this extension, the text you want summarized is sent directly to Google's Gemini API for processing and is not stored by our extension.

Your summary history is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.
